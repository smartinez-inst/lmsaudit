Is it time to re-evaluate your LMS?

This tool will assist you with auditing your current LMS. Rate each question based on your current LMS to visualize your district's pain points and determine if it's time for a new evaluation. You can jump to the different sections by selecting a button below.

Student Experience

Focus: Access, Usability, and Engagement

These questions evaluate the system from the perspective of the primary end-user: the learner. A struggling student experience often leads to low adoption and poor learning outcomes.

Teacher Workflow

Focus: Efficiency, Content Management, and Assessment

These questions address the daily productivity and instructional capacity of teachers. Time spent fighting the LMS is time taken away from instruction.

C&I Leadership

Focus: Pedagogical Alignment and Instructional Data Flow

These questions ensure the LMS supports the district's modern teaching philosophy and provides the deep data necessary for continuous curriculum improvement.

IT Admin Overhead

Focus: System Management, Security, and Scalability

These questions address the strategic, financial, and operational challenges faced by district leadership and IT departments.
